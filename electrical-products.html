Leading Supplier of Premium Branded Products

Kottarakkara.

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Ganesh, Brandz Zone has grown to become one of the largest direct dealers of branded products in Kottarakkara, Kerala. We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of products and services, including electrical, plumbing, sanitary ware, fancy lights, and much more. As a complete interior designing, consulting, and execution firm, our team of talented and efficient designers is dedicated to bringing your vision to life.

At Brandz Zone, we serve a diverse clientele ranging from small local businesses to large national organizations such as housing associations, builders, hospitals, hostels, industries, schools, colleges, and contractors. Our commitment to customer satisfaction and long-lasting relationships has earned us the loyalty of clients across various sectors.

We proudly represent over 130 global brands, including Kohler, Grohe, Jaquar, Hettich, Hafele, and Finolex. Our extensive collection of products is showcased in our showroom, featuring live displays of luxury building materials from top international brands from Germany, Italy, Holland, the US, and France.
